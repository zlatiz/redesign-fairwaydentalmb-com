Meet Dr. David Latham

About Dr. Latham

Dr. Latham grew up in Utah and earned his BA from Brigham Young University in 2003. He is dedicated to providing the highest quality dental care, blending advanced techniques with a compassionate, patient-first approach.

After completing his undergraduate studies, Dr. Latham pursued his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree, where he discovered his passion for cosmetic and restorative dentistry. His commitment to continuing education ensures that Fairway Dental remains at the forefront of modern dental practices in Myrtle Beach.

Outside the office, Dr. Latham enjoys spending time with his family, exploring the beautiful South Carolina coastline, and playing a round of golf whenever he gets the chance.
