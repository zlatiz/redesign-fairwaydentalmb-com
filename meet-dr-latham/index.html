About Dr. Latham

Dr. Latham grew up in Utah and earned his BA from Brigham Young University in 2003. He then attended the University of Louisville School of Dentistry, graduating with his DMD in 2007. Following dental school, he completed a rigorous general practice residency at the VA Hospital in Salt Lake City, gaining advanced training in complex restorative and surgical procedures.

After practicing in Colorado for several years, Dr. Latham and his family relocated to the beautiful coastal community of Myrtle Beach to establish Fairway Dental. His philosophy is centered on patient comfort and comprehensive, conservative care. He believes in educating his patients so they can make informed decisions about their oral health.

When he's not in the office, Dr. Latham enjoys golfing at the many local courses, spending time at the beach with his wife and three children, and participating in local community service initiatives.
