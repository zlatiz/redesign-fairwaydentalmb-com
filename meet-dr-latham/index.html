Meet Dr. David Latham

Exceptional dental care in Myrtle Beach

Dr. David Latham

Lead Dentist

Dr. Latham grew up in Utah and earned his BA from Brigham Young University in 2003. He went on to attend the Medical University of South Carolina College of Dental Medicine, graduating with his Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) degree in 2008.

Following dental school, Dr. Latham completed a General Practice Residency at the University of Utah Hospital, where he received advanced training in complex restorative dentistry, oral surgery, and treating medically compromised patients.

He is passionate about continuing education and stays at the forefront of dental technology to provide his patients with the highest standard of care. Dr. Latham believes in a compassionate, patient-centered approach, ensuring everyone feels comfortable and confident in their treatment. When not in the office, he enjoys exploring the beautiful Myrtle Beach coastline with his family, playing golf, and volunteering with local community health initiatives.
