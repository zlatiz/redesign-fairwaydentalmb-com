About Dr. Latham

Dr. Latham grew up in Utah and earned his BA from Brigham Young University in 2003. He then attended the Medical University of South Carolina (MUSC) in Charleston, earning his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree. Following graduation, he completed a General Practice Residency at Palmetto Health Richland in Columbia, SC, gaining advanced training in various aspects of dentistry.

He is passionate about continuing education and stays current with the latest advancements in dental technology and procedures to provide his patients with the best possible care. Dr. Latham believes in a patient-centered approach, taking the time to listen to concerns and tailoring treatment plans to meet individual needs.

When not in the office, Dr. Latham enjoys spending time with his family, exploring the beautiful outdoors of South Carolina, and playing golf.
