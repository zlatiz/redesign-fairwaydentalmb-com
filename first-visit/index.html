1. Paperwork & Registration

To make your visit as smooth as possible, we encourage you to fill out our new patient forms online before you arrive. If you prefer to do this in person, please arrive 15 minutes early. We'll need a bit of information about your medical history to provide the safest, most tailored care.

medical_information

2. Comprehensive Examination

Your first visit is all about getting to know you, your oral health needs, and your goals. Our dental team will perform a thorough examination, including digital x-rays, a periodontal screening, and a visual inspection of your teeth and gums. We use the latest technology to ensure accurate diagnostics.

forum

3. Consultation & Planning

After the exam, we will sit down with you to discuss our findings. We'll listen to your concerns, answer any questions you might have, and work together to create a personalized treatment plan. We believe in transparency and will explain all your options clearly.
